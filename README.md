# 🚗 RideStats

**Track your rides. Understand your real earnings. Optimize your time on the road.**

RideStats is a lightweight tool built for Uber / Bolt drivers who want clear, structured insights about their work: earnings, hours, and efficiency metrics.

No clutter. No login. Just fast tracking and meaningful reports.

---

## 📊 What RideStats does

RideStats transforms your daily rides into clear performance data:

- 💰 Earnings per day, week, month
- ⏱️ Earnings per hour worked
- 📍 Earnings per kilometer
- 📈 Performance trends over time
- 🧾 Detailed daily breakdowns
- 📅 History and comparisons between days

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 📊 Smart financial tracking
Automatically calculates:
- Total income per day
- Average income per hour
- Efficiency (€/hour, €/km)

### 📅 Daily structure
- Organize rides by day
- See exactly how each day performed
- Compare workdays vs. busy days

### 📈 Personal analytics
- Identify most profitable hours
- Spot low-efficiency periods
- Track improvement over time

### 📶 Offline ready
- Works without internet after first load
- All data stored locally on your device

### 📲 Installable app (PWA)
- Works like a native mobile app
- Fast access directly from home screen

---

## 🚀 How to use

1. Open RideStats in your browser  
2. Add your rides as you complete them  
3. Enter distance and earnings when needed  
4. View automatic daily and hourly stats  
5. Check performance reports anytime  

---

## 📲 Installation

You can install RideStats like a native app:

### Android (Chrome)
- Open the app link  
- Tap **Install app** or **Add to Home Screen**

### iPhone (Safari)
- Open the link  
- Tap **Share → Add to Home Screen**
